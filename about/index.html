About

Cyber Guardian Consulting Group was established in 2015 to offer a much needed, Fortune 100, next-generation Cybersecurity experience for the small- and medium-sized businesses. We quickly evolved to other verticals in order to meet the demand our security customers were requesting. This ranged from software & hardware solutions to low voltage cabling, cameras, business advisory services, backup, Windows, OSX & Linux server administration as well as AWS, Google & Azure cloud management offerings, to name a few. Our belief is that the culture we have built and the people within lend to an extraordinary environment for the success and benefit of our employees, contractors and clients.
