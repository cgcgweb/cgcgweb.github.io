Cyber Guardian Consulting Group (CGCG) is a managed-IT and cybersecurity firm-based in New York. We pride ourselves on the unique culture and values that contributeto an extraordinary environment for employees, contractors, and clients.

Company Culture

CGCG's culture is built on the following guiding principles:

  • Security-first mindset:

    As a cybersecurity firm, CGCG prioritizes a proactive and comprehensive approach to protecting its clients' data and systems. This security-focused culture permeates all aspects of the company's operations.

  • Innovation and problem-solving:

    CGCG encourages its employees to think creatively and collaboratively to develop innovative solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

  • Teamwork and collaboration:

    The company fosters an environment where employees work together as a team, sharing knowledge and expertise to achieve common goals.

  • Continuous learning and growth:

    CGCG values continuous learning and professional development, providing opportunities for employees to expand their skills and knowledge.
