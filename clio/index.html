Clio @ CGCG

CGCG (Cyber Guardian) partners with Clio, a leading cloud-based legal practice management software, to provide comprehensive IT and cybersecurity solutions tailored for law firms.

This collaboration allows CGCG to leverage Clio's robust platform and extensive integrations to streamline law firm operations, enhance productivity, and ensure data security.  By integrating Clio's tools for matter management, document automation, billing, and client communication with CGCG's cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions, law firms can benefit from a seamless and secure digital ecosystem. This partnership enables CGCG to offer law firms a holistic approach, combining Clio's legal-specific features with CGCG's expertise in managed IT services and advanced cybersecurity measures.
