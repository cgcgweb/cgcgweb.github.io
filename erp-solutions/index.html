Our 4-Phase Approach

Data Protection: Implement at-rest and in-transit encryption (TLS/SSL) for all sensitive data and connections.

Access Control: Configure Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) at screen, field, and record levels following the Principle of Least Privilege.

Audit & Monitoring: Enable comprehensive audit logging across critical tables to track activity, record changes, and support compliance.

Vulnerability Management: Run security health checks, perform penetration testing, and ensure timely patching across the ERP application layer.
