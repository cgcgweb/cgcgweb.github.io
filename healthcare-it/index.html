Security, Compliance & Zero-Trust AI Governance

Healthcare innovation must not increase risk. We embed Zero Trust security principles across all AI and EMR integrations—assuming no system, user, or AI component is trusted by default.

Zero Trust Architecture: Continuous identity verification and least-privilege access across users, systems, and AI tools
PHI Protection: End-to-end encryption for all data used by AI models
Human Oversight: Clinicians always review and approve AI-generated content
Access Control: Role-based and context-aware access to patient data
Audit & Compliance: Full audit trails supporting HIPAA, donor, and regulatory reviews
