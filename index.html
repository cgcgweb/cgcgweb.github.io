Questions,
answered.

The six things teams ask before they sign. If yours isn't here, ask us directly — we'll put it on the list.

01

How fast do you respond to critical incidents?

+
Our 24/7 SOC acknowledges P1 incidents within 4 minutes on average. A named incident commander is assigned immediately, with full containment and recovery playbooks running in parallel.
02

Can you co-manage with our existing IT team?

+
Yes. The Secure Growth tier is designed for this. We integrate with your existing ticketing, escalation, and on-call rotations — augmenting where you need help, not replacing people who know your business.
03

What industries do you specialize in?

+
Healthcare, legal, real estate, finance, nonprofit, and construction. Regulated and compliance-heavy environments are our core expertise — HIPAA, SEC, FINRA, and CMMC-aligned practices across the fleet.
04

Do you require long-term contracts?

+
Most engagements run on 12-month terms to align budgets and roadmap, but we offer month-to-month on Essential Operations for smaller teams still finding the right fit.
05

How is pricing structured?

+
Per-seat with transparent add-ons. No mystery line items, no surprise "out of scope" fees. We quote after a discovery audit so the number is real, not a range.
06

What happens if we're breached before onboarding is complete?

+
We run a rapid stabilization pass on day one — MFA, endpoint visibility, and backup verification — so the foundation is defensible within the first week, while the full deployment continues in parallel.
