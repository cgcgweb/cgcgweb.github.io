What This Means For You

You don't need to understand IT.
That's what we're here for.

Managed IT means we become responsible for your technology — not just when something breaks, but all the time. We monitor your systems before problems start, fix issues before your team notices them, and make sure your entire IT environment is always up-to-date, secure, and running at its best.

Think of it this way: instead of calling a plumber every time a pipe bursts, you have someone who checks your pipes regularly, fixes small leaks before they flood the room, and upgrades your system when better options become available. That's what managed IT does for your technology.

Whether you have an internal IT team you want to strengthen (co-managed IT), or no dedicated IT staff at all (fully managed IT), CGCG adapts to your situation. We integrate with your business, learn your environment, and become the IT partner your team actually trusts.

Proactive, not reactive

Most IT companies only show up when something breaks. We monitor your systems continuously and resolve issues before they become outages — often before you even know there was a problem.

Co-managed or fully managed

Already have an IT person or team? We work alongside them. No internal IT at all? We become yours. Either way, you get the same level of coverage and expertise.

One point of contact

No more calling five different vendors for five different problems. CGCG is your single point of contact for your entire technology environment — from a broken printer to a failed server.

How It Works

Getting started is simpler than you think.

We've designed our onboarding process to be transparent, low-disruption, and fast. Here's exactly what happens.

01

Free Assessment

We start with a no-obligation audit of your current IT environment. We look at your network, devices, software, security posture, backup status, and any existing pain points. You get a clear, honest picture of where you stand — with no jargon and no sales pressure.

02

Custom Proposal

Based on the assessment, we build a proposal tailored to your actual environment and business needs. You'll see exactly what's covered, what it costs, and why. No hidden fees. No bloated packages. No services you don't need.

03

Onboarding & Environment Mapping

Our team takes an in-depth inventory of your entire IT environment — every device, every system, every account. We document everything so that when you call us, we already know your setup inside and out.

04

Monitoring & Management Goes Live

We deploy our monitoring and management tools across your environment. From this point forward, we're watching your systems 24/7. Most issues are resolved automatically or before you notice them. For anything that needs your attention, we reach out proactively.

05

Ongoing Partnership

This isn't a set-it-and-forget-it arrangement. We review your environment regularly, recommend improvements, help you plan technology investments, and scale our services as your business grows. You'll always know what's happening and why.
