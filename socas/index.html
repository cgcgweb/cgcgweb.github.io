SOC as a Service (SOCaaS)

CGCG’s Security Operations Center as a Service (SOCaaS) provides an in-depth understanding of what is happening within a network, endpoints and the cloud. These insights empower organizations to respond immediately to any possible threats.

Why do you need a SOCaaS?

Being able to keep up with the ever changing threat landscape is not where a company should place its internal human resources. This is meant for a highly trained and seasoned team, who are hyper-focused on a singular objective – keeping the bad actors out. We specialize in handling APT's, Alerting, Remediation, Emerging Threats, Hunting, Intelligence Digests, Current and Historical Threats, Incident-Based Triage and Hunting, Malware Analysis and Reporting.
