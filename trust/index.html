Chief Information Officer

CIO Advisory Services (CIOaaS)

Every organization, regardless of size, needs someone in charge of its information technology. A Chief Information Officer (CIO) traditionally works to ensure all technology and business interests are aligned to create the best atmosphere possible for achieving your business goals.

Why do you need a CIO?

The CIO coordinates multiple parts of an organization’s operations that require the use of technology. A CGCG CIO will continually partner with your company’s existing IT team or help create one, and then lead your business’s technology strategy. By providing expert advice, a sounding board, and possibly even a sanity check when needed, a CGCG CIO engages in strategic conversations with everyone involved to create the best digital environment possible.

Our CIOaaS is a flexible model ensuring the service meets your needs while providing the best value. This partnership is created based on needs, goals and KPI's. From small projects such as helping with IT policy work which could take a few days, to an oversight role that requires a couple of days a month, to large business projects which may require full-time service for a few months, our CIO works with you to assess, plan and complete any IT related tasks.

CIO manages:

Data privacy

We constantly perform a system-wide evaluation to be sure all internal data isn't public facing, exposed by employees and not maliciously shared.

Uptime/reliability

Advanced monitoring and visibility is our main task after hardening systems against downtime.

Budgetary constrains

Our work and structure is always on-time and on-budget.

Disaster recovery

In a case of mass failure, we protect against water, fire, mass electrical and ISP outages, war, amongst others, to account for proactive solutions, planning, and recovery.

Staffing

Ensuring the right employees stay and the laggards are replaced with a breath of fresh air.

Compliance

We carry industry best compliances, we make sure your do as well.

Data management

We oversee data on-premise, hybrid, and in the cloud. This is in addition to third-party and off-site, off-cloud managed data sets.

Scalability

In today's world we must be prepared for unprecedented upticks in volume. Whether this be a website, servers, e-mails, etc.. we account for these and design systems to be able to move forward as your business does.
